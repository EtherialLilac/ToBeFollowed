WHY DONATE TO CPA?

Your donation to the Companion Pet Adoption Management will immediately impact the lives of the to be abandon animals, abused, or neglected animals by providing needed medical care, food, shelter, and safety. You can donate in confidence that every penny is utilized responsibly. As the newest establish organization CPA strive its live-release goals. Please, help us save more homeless pets by making a donation today!

Click to donate
These donations go a long way in providing the needs of our partnership shelter animals. Below is a list of items that they need on a daily basis:

Pets Wish List


• Dog and Cat food (kibble and canned)
• Crates, carriers or cages
• Pet diapers and wee wee pads
• Scratching posts for the cats
• Chew toys for the dogs
• Vaccines, medicine and vitamins
• Dog and cat treats
• Leashes, harnesses and collars


Shelter Wish List


• Detergent powder and bleach
• Dishwashing paste or liquid
• Bath towels
• Garbage bags (XXL)
• Foot rugs or door mats
• Clinic supplies (alcohol, cotton, etc.)
• Old newspapers
• Toilet paper
• Mops and brooms
