HOW CPA OPERATES

The Companion Pet Adoption is supported through donations, grants, requests from individuals, and special events to raise needed funds. Directed by a volunteer board and operated by a professional management. Through grassroots advocacy, and education, the Companion Pet Adoption strives to be the leading voice for unwanted pets in crisis.

MISSION


The Companion Pet Adoption System is a website platform that works to find a new home for your furry friend. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for these animals and give them chance to find a new companion by posting it online and endorsing it to our organizations partners and people.

VISSION


Our Vision is a world where every companion animal has a safe and loving forever home.
